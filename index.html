Privacy Policy – Timer for Life

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

Timer for Life values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and handled when you use the Timer for Life mobile application. We are committed to transparency and user trust.

1. Information We Collect

Timer for Life does not directly collect, store, or manage personal user data on its own servers.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect limited information as required for their functionality.

2. Third-Party Services Used

a) Advertising (Google AdMob)

Timer for Life displays ads using Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (such as device type and OS version)
  • Approximate location (country or city level)
  • Ad interaction data (such as ad views and clicks)

This data is used by Google to provide and improve advertising services.

b) In-App Purchases (Google Play Billing)

Timer for Life uses Google Play Billing to process in-app purchases and subscriptions.

Google may collect and process:

  • Purchase history
  • Subscription status
  • Transaction-related information

Timer for Life does not collect or store payment details, such as credit card numbers or bank information. All payments are securely handled by Google Play.

3. How the App Works

  • Core timer functionality works offline.
  • Timer data, preferences, and settings are stored locally on the user’s device.
  • No personal data is transmitted to the developer’s servers.

4. Data Sharing

Timer for Life does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Any data collected is handled directly by Google services (AdMob and Google Play Billing) in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy.

Learn more here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. Analytics, Advertising & Tracking

  • The app includes advertising through Google AdMob.
  • The app does not use custom analytics tools.
  • Any tracking is performed only by Google services as required for ads and billing.

6. App Permissions

Timer for Life only requests permissions necessary for:

  • Basic timer functionality
  • Displaying advertisements (as required by Google services)

The app does not request access to contacts, photos, media files, or sensitive personal information.

7. Children’s Privacy

Timer for Life is intended for general audiences.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Advertising shown complies with Google Play policies.

8. Data Security

Timer for Life does not store personal user data on external servers.

All third-party data processing is secured by Google’s infrastructure and security standards.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s features or services change. Any updates will be reflected within the app or its store listing.
